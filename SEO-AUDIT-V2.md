# RankReady — Full SEO / Offer Audit (Internal)

## Executive verdict
RankReady has a decent landing page skeleton, but it is **not yet operating at the standard of the $199 service it sells**.

Right now the site is strongest at:
- pain articulation
- simple pricing comprehension
- fast free-audit interaction

Right now it is weakest at:
- trust / credibility
- local SEO depth on-page
- conversion proof
- offer clarity vs alternatives
- true search capture beyond the homepage

## Biggest issues

### 1. Headline is generic and risky
"Get Found on Google. Guaranteed." creates legal / trust issues and sounds like hype.

### 2. No real trust layer
No case studies, no real screenshots, no founder credibility, no process proof, no sample report preview, no deliverable examples.

### 3. Homepage SEO is thin
There is no real long-form search-intent capture for:
- local SEO audit
- google business profile optimization
- local SEO services for small businesses
- citations / directory cleanup
- AI search readiness

### 4. Pricing may be mismatched
The $199 offer is solid, but the monthly pricing likely feels expensive without trust proof.
The Launch plan at $499 may also be too feature-heavy for a cold visitor.

### 5. Free audit is strong as interaction, weak as conversion funnel
It produces a nice-looking report but does not deeply bridge into:
- sample deliverables
- why RankReady is better
- risk reversal
- what happens after payment

### 6. Homepage lacks conversion support sections
Missing or weak:
- sample deliverables
- industries served
- FAQ objections
- process detail
- founder / operator credibility
- guarantee/risk-reversal clarification

## Package / pricing recommendation
### Keep
- $199 anchor offer

### Change
- Growth: **$299/mo** instead of $349/mo
- Launch: **$399/mo** instead of $499/mo

### Why
The site does not yet justify premium-ish recurring pricing. Lower monthly tiers reduce friction and improve first-client close odds.

## Offer recommendation
### $199 plan should be framed as
**Local SEO Audit + Action Plan**
Not "audit + fix everything"

Reason: the current promise feels broad. Better to overdeliver.

### Revised packaging
#### Starter Audit — $199 one-time
- Full local SEO audit
- GBP audit + optimization recommendations
- website SEO findings
- citation / NAP scan
- competitor snapshot
- 30-minute strategy call
- top-priority fix plan

#### Growth — $299/mo
- everything from audit
- monthly GBP optimization support
- 4 GBP posts/month
- review monitoring + response drafts
- monthly reporting
- citation monitoring

#### Pro / Launch — $399/mo
- everything in Growth
- on-page website SEO changes
- schema implementation
- local landing page recommendations/buildout support
- expanded directory work
- quarterly strategy call
