Priority Fix Plan — What a Real Deliverable Looks Like

This is the kind of specific, ranked action plan you receive — not generic advice. Names and data are illustrative.

1

Complete your GBP business description

Current: 48 characters. Target: 750+ characters with local keywords. Estimated impact: +20–35% profile views within 3 weeks of update.

2

Add 3 secondary GBP business categories

Your competitors average 4 categories. Each additional category expands the searches you appear for. Implementation time: ~10 minutes in GBP dashboard.

3

Fix NAP inconsistency on Yelp and Apple Maps

Your address format differs across 3 major directories. Google cross-references these — inconsistency reduces trust and suppresses rankings.

4

Set up a post-visit review request system

You have 12 reviews; your top local competitor has 84. A simple QR code or follow-up text request typically adds 3–6 reviews per month with no paid tools required.

5

Add LocalBusiness schema markup to your homepage

Missing structured data means AI assistants (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity) cannot reliably surface your business in responses. Fix takes about 1 hour to implement.
